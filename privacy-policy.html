Privacy Policy

Last updated:

3 PIN Realty ("we", "us") provides a WhatsApp-based assistant and CRM to help customers find real estate listings and follow up with leads. This policy explains what data we collect and how it's used.

What we collect

  • WhatsApp conversations: messages you send to our WhatsApp number, so our team (and an AI assistant) can respond and follow up.
  • Contact details: name, phone number, and anything you share about your property requirements (area, budget, configuration).
  • Lead-form submissions: if you submit a Facebook/Instagram lead ad, the details you provided in that form.

How we use it

To respond to your enquiry, match you with relevant listings, and have a team member follow up. Messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to draft replies; data is stored using Google Firebase/Firestore.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. It's shared only with the service providers above (Meta/WhatsApp for messaging, Anthropic for AI processing, Google Firebase for storage) strictly to provide this service.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your data at any time by contacting us below.
